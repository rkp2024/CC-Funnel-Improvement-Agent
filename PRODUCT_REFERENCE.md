## Application Process (10 Minutes)

### Information Required:
- Aadhaar-linked mobile number
- Email address
- PAN number

### Steps:

#### Step 1: Basic Information Registration
- Mobile number, device binding, email, PAN details
- **Note:** Existing Jupiter app users can skip this

#### Step 2: PAN Card Confirmation
- Enter PAN for instant verification
- **Reason:** RBI mandate for identity, credit bureau reporting, tax compliance

#### Step 3: Eligibility Check
**Details Required:**
- Date of Birth
- PinCode (serviceability check)
- Professional details (education, organization)
- CIBIL consent
- Personal details (parents' names)
- Marital status
- Income details

**Impact:** No impact on credit score (soft inquiry)

#### Step 4: Card CVP & Limit Assignment
- Credit limit assigned (₹25,000 to ₹7,00,000)

#### Step 5: Personal Details Form
- Complete name, address, employment details

#### Step 6: eKYC Process (Aadhaar)
**Requirements:**
- Aadhaar number (not physical card)
- Liveliness check
- Address confirmation

**Process:** UIDAI regulated, RBI approved, takes seconds

**Reason:** 100% paperless KYC compliance per RBI norms

#### Step 7: vKYC Process
**Requirements:**
- Well-lit, quiet place
- Physical PAN card
- Stable internet connection
- White blank paper and pen
- Remember application details

**Approval Time:** Up to 24 hours after completion

#### Step 8: Virtual Card & UPI Setup
- Enable virtual card
- Set up UPI
- Start using immediately!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is PAN mandatory?
PAN is required as per RBI regulations for:
- Identity verification
- Credit bureau reporting to CIBIL
- Tax compliance
- Financial transaction tracking

### Why is Aadhaar needed?
Aadhaar enables:
- Instant eKYC (Electronic Know Your Customer)
- 100% paperless verification
- RBI-compliant KYC process
- UIDAI-regulated secure verification

Only the 12-digit number is needed, not the physical card.

### How long does approval take?
- Eligibility check: Instant
- eKYC: Few seconds
- vKYC approval: Up to 24 hours
- Overall: Application can be completed in ~10 minutes

### Do UPI rewards work with any app?
Rewards are credited **ONLY** when UPI transactions are made via the **Jupiter App**. While you can use any UPI app, cashback is exclusive to Jupiter App transactions.
